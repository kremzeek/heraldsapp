
Cookies
=======

The app needs 5 cookies:

* gaia55_autologin
* gaia55_tag
* gaia55_prp
* gaia55_authpath
* gaia55_sid

The app suggests sample formats for them.

Pull them up from your browser sesssion. 

For Chrome, go to the Chrome Menu, then select settings.  Go to Privacy, then Cookies.

For Firefox, go to Options -> Privacy -> View Cookies.

In Internet Explorer, go Tools, Internet Options.  Then Browsing History -> Settings.  Then View Objects.
